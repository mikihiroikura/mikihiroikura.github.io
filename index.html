Biography
Mikihiro Ikura is a Ph.D student in Graduate School of Engineering, Department of Precision Engineering, the University of Tokyo. My major is high speed image processing, 3D sensing, and Robotics at Asama Yamashita Laboratory. In particular, my research is focused on an adaptive 3D sensing system with adjustable spatio-temporal resolution and measurement in real time, and a real-time landing gear control system for UAV safe landing on an unknown and unstable ground.
